Hi Rubyists,  
the summer has arrived, so it's time for another relaxed gathering with Ruby fellows! We'll be meeting at our standard drinkup place, Pivnica Medvedgrad in Ilica, to enjoy some good drinks and even better conversations.
  
This time, we're also happy to welcome our friends from JSZG, so it's a perfect opportunity to meet developers from both communities.

See you in Pivnica Medvedgrad!
